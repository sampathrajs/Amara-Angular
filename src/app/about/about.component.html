About Us

Arihant Amara Residents Association (AARA) was registered under the Tamilnadu Societies Act and became functional effective 11th November 2013.

AARA is managed by nine elected Committee Members, viz., a President, a Secretary, a Treasurer, a joint Secretary, a Vice-president and four other committee members.

AARA's office is managed by an Office Manager and a Facility Manager, both on full-time basis. The office is open from 10.00 am to 6.00 pm on all working days to attend to the needs of the owners and residents. Land line - 004-4952 5005.
